Thalpy updated:

  • fixes cold reactions not reacting in a few limited cases

necromanceranne updated:

  • Shielded Robes and Mirror Shields can no longer be made from daemon forges.
  • Eldritch Longswords and True Nar'sian Hardened Armor can be made from daemon forges. True Nar'sian Hardened Armor has no slowdown, unlike the lavaland version.
  • The equipment spell now grants you a cult dagger. Still separate from the other spell so that you can acquire a dagger without being slapped with a validsuit.
  • Eldritch longswords and cult daggers have block chance (50% and 25% respectively). Cult daggers cannot block projectiles and the cult ghost longsword only has 25% block chance.
  • The cult blindfold only stuns you if you actually are stupid enough to put it over your eyes.
  • Fixes the halberd not correctly displaying the correct inhand sprites.

vincentiusvin updated:

  • atmos devices with multiple ends that is hooked to the same line can now redistribute pressure more consistently. (can connect to a single pipeline in more than one nodes)
